SUMMARY                                         *open-rubygems*

Easily open the rubygems.org page for gem from Vim.

                                                *open-rubygems-:Gem*
:Gem [{gem-name}]     Use :Gem rails to open up rails on rubygems.org.
                      If the cursor is located on a gem name you can
                      use :Gem to open up the rubygems.org page for that gem.
